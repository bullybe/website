Educational Websites for Children

Khan Academy

  • Lessons are taught similar to school format.
  • Rewards in the program are available but minimal.
  • Math section adheres to common core standards. Other sections are not as well developed.

ABC Mouse / Adventure Academy

  • ABCMouse provides a full curriculum enhancement for children prek to first grade.
          Adveture Academy is a new release from the same company for children 8-13.
  • Both programs offer a complex and immersive rewards system.
  • Lessons feel more like a video game than a typical instructor-led explanation and exercise.

IXL

  • Like Khan Academy, IXL provides content to all levels of learners, including prek-12 and ESL.
  • A rewards program is used for motivation, but it is not so detailed as ABC Mouse.
  • Provides tools for instructors and parents to monitor and assign specific lessons.
